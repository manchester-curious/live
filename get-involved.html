Want to get involved?

A wide range of volunteers with a variety of skills are required to make an event like Manchester Curious a success.

We are currently looking for team members to join the committee to help drive the festival forward and assist with delivering the 2016 festival.

If you are interested in becoming involved in Manchester Curious 2016 please get in touch. All are welcome.

Building and Tour Hosts

Over 90% of the tickets were booked for the inaugural 2015 festival and we are looking to build on this success in 2016.

We are on the lookout for spaces and tours for our 2016 programme, these include:

  • Interesting and innovative buildings
  • Hidden spaces not usually open to the public
  • New developments or sites that can be toured
  • Walking tour guides

We would love to hear from you if you have a building or tour that you think would be suitable for Manchester Curious, info@manchestercurious.com.

Any suggestions of buildings or tours that you would like to see on the programme are also welcome.

Sponsorship

We have a variety of sponsorship packaged available. Please email info@manchestercurious.com if you would be interested in sponsoring this exciting, engaging festival.
